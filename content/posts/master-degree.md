Today I experienced a very special moment: the defense of my master’s dissertation.

Entering the academic and scientific world close to the age of 50 was an enchanting, challenging, and deeply transformative experience. Throughout this journey, I learned so much from every professor, from my colleagues, from the examination committee, and, above all, from the opportunity to conduct research that allowed me to look at professional practice with greater method, depth, and scientific curiosity.

I close this chapter with deep gratitude to everyone who was part of this path, especially Ivanilton Polato, Fabio Kon, Reginaldo Ré, Igor Wiese, and André Luis Schwerz, and with the certainty that it is never too late to learn.

And after this journey… who knows, maybe in a few years I will be back here to share the next milestone: a PhD graduation.
